# APA7 Quick Reference

## In-Text Citations

### Narrative (author as subject)

| Authors | First Citation | Subsequent |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| 1 author | Maguire (2024) | Maguire (2024) |
| 2 authors | Deci and Ryan (2017) | Deci and Ryan (2017) |
| 3+ authors | Gagné et al. (2015) | Gagné et al. (2015) |

### Parenthetical (citation at end)

| Authors | Format |
|---------|--------|
| 1 author | (Maguire, 2024) |
| 2 authors | (Deci & Ryan, 2017) |
| 3+ authors | (Gagné et al., 2015) |

### Multiple Works in One Citation

- Same parentheses, separated by semicolons, alphabetical order:
  (Bandura, 1997; Deci & Ryan, 2017; Dweck, 2006)

### Direct Quotes

- Include page number: (Author, Year, p. 12)
- Block quote (40+ words): indented, no quotation marks, citation after full stop

### Secondary Sources

- Cite the secondary source: (as cited in Author, Year)
- List only the secondary source in references
- Use sparingly — always prefer the original

## Reference List Entries

### Journal Article

Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name*, *volume*(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xxxxx

Example:
Deci, E. L., & Ryan, R. M. (2000). The "what" and "why" of goal pursuits: Human needs and the self-determination of behaviour. *Psychological Inquiry*, *11*(4), 227-268. https://doi.org/10.1207/S15327965PLI1104_01

### Book

Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of book* (edition if not first). Publisher.

Example:
Dweck, C. S. (2006). *Mindset: The new psychology of success*. Random House.

### Edited Book Chapter

Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. E. Editor (Ed.), *Title of book* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher.

### Conference Paper

Author, A. A. (Year, Month days). *Title of paper*. Conference Name, Location. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Web Source

Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of page*. Site Name. https://url

### Preprint

Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of preprint*. Repository Name. https://doi.org/xxxxx

Example:
Smith, J. (2024). *Transformer architectures for temporal data*. arXiv. https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2401.12345

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Correction |
|---------|-----------|
| Using "et al." for 2-author works | Always name both authors for 2-author works |
| Omitting DOI when available | Include DOI for all works that have one |
| Using "&" in narrative citations | Use "and" in narrative, "&" in parenthetical |
| Inconsistent capitalisation in titles | Sentence case for article titles, title case for journal names |
| Missing issue number | Include issue number in parentheses after volume |
| Citing a source not in references | Every in-text citation must have a corresponding reference entry |
| Reference entry with no in-text citation | Remove from reference list or add citation in text |
| Using "p." for paraphrases | Page numbers required only for direct quotes |

## Formatting Notes

- Reference list: hanging indent, double-spaced, alphabetical by first author surname
- Italicise: journal names, book titles, volume numbers
- Do not italicise: article titles, chapter titles, issue numbers
- Use sentence case for article and chapter titles
- Use title case for journal and book titles
